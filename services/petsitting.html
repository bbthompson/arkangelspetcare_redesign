Pet Sitting

Our clients trust us to handle even the most challenging situations because we carefully review all of your pet's needs during our initial consultation. Our in home pet sitting staff is experienced and knowledgeable in the care of dogs, cats, birds, and exotics. In addition our staff is experienced in administering insulin and subcutaneous fluids as needed.

Ark Angels Pet Care visits are tailored to your specific needs.

Services provided include but are not limited to:

feeding and fresh water
exercise/playtime
medication administration
pet waste disposal
focused love and attention
brushing
pet housekeeping
post-visit updates with photos
mail & newspaper pickup
plant watering
blinds and lights rotation
garbage day curbside service

Pet Sitting Consultation Checklist

We have prepared this checklist to help you plan for talking with our in home pet sitting representative:

  • What is the daily routine of your pet?
  • Does your pet require any medications?
    • What kind?
    • What time(s) of day is the medication administered?
  • Are there additional procedures that need to be followed (or are helpful)?
  • Information relating to the personality of your pet is very valuable.
    • Is your pet shy?
    • Does your pet have separation anxiety?
    • Is your pet aggressive?
    • Does your pet tend to get into trouble when alone?
  • What, if anything, scares your pet or triggers aggressive behavior?
  • Does your pet have any unusual habits?
  • Does your pet wear a set of identification tags?
  • Is there a guardian designated for your pet in case of an unfortunate event?
    • If so please provide a name and contact for this individual.
