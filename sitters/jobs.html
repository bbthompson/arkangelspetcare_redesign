We Are Hiring

Ark Angels Pet Care provides security and peace of mind to our clients by caring for their animals and their homes while they are away. This is the perfect job for healthy retirees, empty nesters or working professionals able to work flexible schedules. Ark Angels Pet Care is busy and we need passionate, dependable and knowledgeable sitters.

Please review the application requirements below and note that only resumes with cover letters will receive a reply.

We are currently hiring sitters that live and can work in the following zip codes:

  • North Raleigh (27609, 27612, 27613, 27614, and 27615)
  • West Raleigh, Brier Creek area (27612, 27617)
  • Raleigh (27606, 27607)
  • Cary (27511, 27512, 27513, 27518, and 27519)
  • Apex (27502, 27523, and 27539)
  • Morrisville (27560)

Job Requirements

  • Able to commit to a minimum of 1 year
  • Available every other weekend and holiday
  • Working knowledge and experience with dogs and cats
  • Administering pills or topical medications is not required but a plus
  • Have dependable transportation and smart phone
  • Available and flexible during the following hours:
    • 6:30am - 8:00am
    • 11:00am - 2:00pm (midday dog walk clients)
    • 5:00pm - 7:00pm
    • 8:00pm - 9:30pm (occassional bedtime visits)
  • Able to pass a criminal background and DMV check
  • Able to work independently
  • Professional and Reliable demeanor
  • Excellent communication skills

This is a part time, contracted position.

Email resume and cover letter to arkangelspetcare@gmail.com.
