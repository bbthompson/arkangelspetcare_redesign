Why Hire A Professional Pet Sitting Service?

Animals love familiarity and routine. They are creatures of habit. Pets can become stressed when moved out of their comfort zone. They are the happiest and healthiest when life is straightforward and familiar. Hiring our pet sitting service means your pet can stay in the comfort of your home and the convenience of their usual routine while you are away.

We take pet sitting very seriously. We understand that each pet is unique — that’s why we ask detailed questions about your pets and the care they require, and we carefully follow the specific instructions provided. When you hire Ark Angels pet sitting service, your pet receives attention from a trained professional with extensive experience in providing care, feeding, grooming and even administering medications when needed. Pet owners who hire us appreciate the peace of mind that comes from knowing their pets are receiving excellent, expert care when they cannot be there themselves.
